About WristReward

WristReward is built on a simple idea: if your attention has value to advertisers, you should share in that value.

Our patent-pending biometric verification system is designed to use heart rate variability, galvanic skin response, motion detection, and temperature sensing to prove genuine human attention - something no click or view count can do.

We're working toward a future where the attention economy benefits everyone, not just platforms. Currently in development.
